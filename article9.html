HUM AFSAANE HAI

Wese to hum kalakaar hai,
Par hum par to aapka adhikaar hai,
Hum kahaniya or nagme prastut karte hai,
Par aakhir ye aapke hi taraane hai,
Hum afsaane hai !

Hum jindagi ke ache bure pal dikhate hai
Kabhi khud ro kar aapko hasate hai,
Agar aap koi Bollywood movie hai,
To hum aapke ho gaane hai,
Hum afssane hai !

Humara natak hasata hai, rulata hai,
To kabhi koi pyara sandesh de jata hai,
Humare kuch drashya kalpanik or nye hai,
Or kuch sacche or puraane hai
Hum afssane hai !

Humare har pal me alag manoranjan hai,
Is kalpanik sacchai me aapka abhinandan hai,
Is samaj ke ache - sunder chehre bhi batane hai,
Hume isi samaj ke kuch kaale – ghahre roop bhi dikhane hai,
Hum afsaane hai !

Humara uddeshya apko hasana – rulana hai,
Kuch batana, kuch samjana hai,
Jese jindagi uthati – girati hai, apne isharo par nachati hai,
Par aakhir ye bhi to ek “ Afsaana ” hai.

~ Sanjay Singh
