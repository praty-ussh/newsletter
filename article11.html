Engineering Kya Hai?

Enginnering in 4 saalo me dosto ki yaari hai,
Agar kismat achi ho to ek sunder naari hai,
College ki 75% attendance bhi lagati bhaari hai,
Jab hostel me raho, to ghar jaldi jaane ki bekarari hai.

Enginnering me baaki to pata nhi par management ke lesson roj lagte,
Assignment, file, project likh-likh kar hath bhi majduro jese hote,
Jab class jaane ka mann nhi hota to, dost hi proxy lagaya karte,
Yaaro engineering me ladkiya me aasani se nhi milti,
Hum to apni imagination me hi khoye rahte hai.

Yaha par kai tarah ke log milte hai,
Kuch ache to kuch ajeeb lagte hai,
“kuch nhi padha yaar” bol kar bhi top karte hai,
Or kuch hum jese, jinda lash banke rahte hai.

Engineering Pappu ki wo machine hai jisme aalo se sona banta hai,
In 4 saalo ka struggle baad me acha lagta hai,
Ho sakta hai abhi safar thoda mushkil lage, par
Engineering wo koyle ki khan hai jis’se heera nikalta hai.

~ Sanjay Singh
