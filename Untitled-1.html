BEAUTY REDIFENED

When you first hear this word , what comes to your mind
blue eyes, rosy lips, dimples , fair skin but this is the
most faulty perception of beauty that one can hold.
Beauty lies in the sacrifice which parents make to give a
life they have dreamt of to their children.
Beauty lies within the soul of that teenage girl who had
the guts to fight for herself and stand alone after her
modesty had been outraged .
Beauty lies in the thought process of that old palliative
man who himself not being able to make both ends meet
still tries to gather resources to provide aid to the
suffering and the poor.
Beauty lies in the spirit of that single mother who despite
being chained down by so many patriarchal norms is not
breaking down and working to hold herself and her child
together.
Beauty lies in the courage shown by that school girl
when she stood up against her bullies.
Beauty lies in the fact the country of the whites was
headed by a back person.
Beauty lies in the fact that some people can be so
ostensibly humble that they do not want to be praised
even after doing so much for the society and everyone
around them.
You know what else beauty lies in ;
it lies within you ;
you should just have the power to accept yourself ;
be a better version of yourself everyday and not be a football
of others’ opinions.

~ Meghna Singh

ताकत को मोहब्बत न कर तू, मोहब्बत कि ताकत को समझ तू ।। आज अहंकार टूटा है तेरा, पहले उसको संभाल तू । क्या गुनाह था उस बेटी का, जिसकी आशाओं को तूने जला दिया ।। क्या गुनाह था उस पुत्री का, जिसके मुखड़े को तूने घुंघट में छुपा दिया । क्यों समझता है तू‌, औरत को दूसरे दर्जे का इंसान ।। आए दिन कुचल देता है, उनका छोटा सा आसमान । बुजदिल है क्या तू, जो करता है तेज़ाब से वार ।। इनकार हि किया था उसने, क्यों नहीं कर पाया तू स्वीकार । बढ़ी हिम्मत से वो लड़ी होगी, बढ़े दर्दों से वो जूझी होगी ।। अपनों को परखने का मौका मिला होगा, फिर से जिंदगी से रूबरू हुई होगी । भारी हो जाता है मन, ये सब सुनके ।। शर्मिंदा हो जाता है दिल, इस हिंसात्मक मर्दानगी से । अब बस इंसाफ कि गुहार है अदालत से ।। ~ Guru.

~ अनीशा

~ अनीशा
