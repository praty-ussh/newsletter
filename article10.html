Exams

Jindagi achi chal rahi thi or exams aa gaye ,
Abhi to padhna shuru bhi nhi kiya or exams aa gaye |

Na time-table ka kuch pata tha, na thi syllabus ki koi khabhar,
Bas paper dene chale gaye, aage wale ke bharose par,
Exam me paper itna tough aaya, ek code bhi na bna paya,
Invigilator bhi kamina aaya, usne bhi bahot sataya,
Socha ab to bahot padhunga, next exam me faad dunga,
Room par aaya, pen uthaya, kithab kholi, par kuch samaj na aaya.

Thoda hua bechain, thoda ghabraya, fir hua ek ahsaas,
Dost apna topper hai, ab wahi karveyega pass.
Jese tese dost ne padhaya, thoda-thoda samaj me aaya,
Paper dekh kar watt lag gayi, exam me kuch likh nhi paya.

Hum raat-raat bhar jag kar syllabus complete karte hai,
Agle din aadhi nind me jakar bhi paper likhte hai,
Paper bura ho to bahot dukh hota hai, lekin
Dost ka bhi bura ho to sath me bhi haste hai.

Bas isi tarah exams ka silsila chalta rahta hai,
Har baar ek naya lesson, naya experience milta rahta hai,
Exams ka khauf or uski tension to hoti rahti hai
Par ghar jaane ki khushi me dil hasta rahta hai.

~ Sanjay Singh
