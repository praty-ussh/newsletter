Kya Kasoor Thaa

Kya kusoor thaa

Kya kusoor thaa un baccho kaa jinki laashein aaj sad rahi hain ,
Kyun nafrat ki lapte din-ba-din badh rahi hain .

Kya kusoor thaa un logon kaa jo aman pasand kara-karte the ,
Dushmani nahi hai aachi , Bujurg theek kaha karte the .

Kyaa kusoor thaa un parindo ka jo subha daana dhundne nikle honge ,
Chuze jinke shayad abi bhi intazaar kar rahe honge.

Kya kusoor tha un masoomo ka jo school mei padhne gaye,
Sapne the jinke adhoore se , aaj khala me dafn ho gaye.

Kisi ko doctor banna tha to kisi ko engineer apna jeevan
unhone sayaad hi abhi jiya tha
Lekin nafrat ki maar ne sabko rakh bana dia tha .

Karodo yade rahi hongi ,un yaadon ko bhulana aasan hai ?
Insan apne hi aakrosh ko kabu karne mei Nakaam hai.

Kya ho gaya insaan ko ,khud ko khuda samajh baitha hai .
Bhool chuka hai ki kon paraya hai or kon apna caheta hai .

Lakhon massomon ko maara , mauka mila hota tab to vo baccha rota,
Shayad pattharon ko kabi dard mahsoos hi nahi hota .

Jihaad ke naam pe unhone hazaaron ki jaane lii hogi,
Masoomo ki kurbaani par kya khaak khuda ko taskeen mili hogi .

~ Saurabh Singh
