khabar balatkar ki

Aao ek baat btau,
Aaj sahi mein sab ko kuch likh ke sunau..

Mere desh ka naam pure sansar mein hua hain..
Sala, aaj yahan firse balatkar hua hain...

Ek maa ne toh usko phoolon ki taraf paala tha,
Us darinde ka usne kya hi bigaada tha...

Dozakh mein bhi jagah na mile us darinde ko,
Aaj Mere desh ne alvida kaha ek aur "Nirbhaya" ko...

Ek baap ne toh usko raani bna ke rkha tha,
Us darinde ne to use kakh bna ke rkh diya...

Abhi toh sihaayi sukhi bhi nhi thi akhbaar ki,
Lo ek aur khabar aagyi balatkar ki..

~ SB
